Rental Agreement Summary

All rentals require a signed agreement outlining the duration, fees, and responsibilities. Set-up and breakdown are included in the rental fee unless stated otherwise.

Cancellation & Refund Policy

Cancellations made 7 days prior to the event will receive a full refund. Cancellations within 7 days will incur a 25% cancellation fee. Same-day cancellations are non-refundable.

Clients are responsible for ensuring safe operating conditions at the event site. We carry general liability insurance. A waiver may be required for all participants depending on the event type and venue.
